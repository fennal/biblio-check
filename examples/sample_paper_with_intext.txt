Introduction

The transformer architecture has reshaped modern NLP. The seminal work introducing self-attention was Vaswani et al. (2017), and subsequent surveys built on this foundation (LeCun et al., 2015). The framework for human judgment under uncertainty (Tversky & Kahneman, 1974) has been foundational in behavioral economics. A separate line of work explored CRISPR-Cas systems, beginning with the dual-RNA-guided endonuclease characterization (Jinek et al., 2012).

Recent work by Patel and Sharma (2022) attempted to extend the transformer to multimodal inputs. However, see also the contrasting view expressed by Smith et al. (2019), which we engage with at length below.

Methods

Following Vaswani et al. (2017), we use multi-head attention with eight heads.
